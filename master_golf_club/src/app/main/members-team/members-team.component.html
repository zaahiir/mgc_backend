Club Protocol

Dress Code Policies & Clubhouse Info

Our golf club maintains the highest standards of elegance and professionalism. We believe that proper attire enhances the overall experience for all members and guests, creating an atmosphere of respect and tradition.

  • Tank tops, t-shirts, and athletic shorts are not permitted
  • Golfers may wear sweaters, vests, or windbreakers
  • Collared shirts while on the course
  • Soft spikes or spikeless golf shoes required
  • Proper golf attire in all club facilities
